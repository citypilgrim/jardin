1.  The nations, O God, have invaded Your inheritance;they have defiled Your holy templeand reduced Jerusalem to rubble. A Psalm of Asaph.
2.  They have given the corpses of Your servantsas food to the birds of the air,the flesh of Your saints to the beasts of the earth. A Psalm of Asaph.
3.  They have poured out their blood like waterall around Jerusalem,and there is no one to bury the dead. A Psalm of Asaph.
4.  We have become a reproach to our neighbors,a scorn and derision to those around us.  A Psalm of Asaph.
5.  How long, O LORD?Will You be angry forever?Will Your jealousy burn like fire? A Psalm of Asaph.
6.  Pour out Your wrath on the nationsthat do not acknowledge You,on the kingdomsthat refuse to call on Your name, A Psalm of Asaph.
7.  for they have devoured Jacoband devastated his homeland.  A Psalm of Asaph.
8.  Do not hold past sins against us;let Your compassion come quickly,for we are brought low. A Psalm of Asaph.
9.  Help us, O God of our salvation,for the glory of Your name;deliver us and atone for our sins,for the sake of Your name. A Psalm of Asaph.
10. Why should the nations ask,“Where is their God?”Before our eyes, make known among the nationsYour vengeance for the bloodshed of Your servants.  A Psalm of Asaph.
11. May the groans of the captives reach You;by the strength of Your arm preserve those condemned to death. A Psalm of Asaph.
12. Pay back into the laps of our neighborssevenfold the reproach they hurled at You, O Lord. A Psalm of Asaph.
13. Then we Your people, the sheep of Your pasture,will thank You forever;from generation to generationwe will declare Your praise.
