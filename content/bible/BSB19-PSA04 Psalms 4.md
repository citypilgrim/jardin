1.  Answer me when I call,O God of my righteousness!You have relieved my distress;show me grace and hear my prayer. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of David.
2.  How long, O men, will my honor be maligned?How long will you love vanity and seek after lies?SelahFor the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of David.
3.  Know that the LORD has set apart the godly for Himself;the LORD hears when I call to Him. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of David.
4.  Be angry, yet do not sin;on your bed, search your heart and be still.SelahFor the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of David.
5.  Offer the sacrifices of the righteousand trust in the LORD. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of David.
6.  Many ask, “Who can show us the good?”Shine the light of Your face upon us, O LORD.For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of David.
7.  You have filled my heart with more joythan when grain and new wine abound.For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of David.
8.  I will lie down and sleep in peace,for You alone, O LORD, make me dwell in safety.
