1.  Ascribe to the LORD, O heavenly beings,ascribe to the LORD glory and strength.A Psalm of David.
2.  Ascribe to the LORD the glory due His name;worship the LORD in the splendor of His holiness. A Psalm of David.
3.  The voice of the LORD is over the waters;the God of glory thunders;the LORD is heard over many waters.A Psalm of David.
4.  The voice of the LORD is powerful;the voice of the LORD is majestic.A Psalm of David.
5.  The voice of the LORD breaks the cedars;the LORD shatters the cedars of Lebanon.A Psalm of David.
6.  He makes Lebanon skip like a calf,and Sirion like a young wild ox. A Psalm of David.
7.  The voice of the LORDstrikes with flames of fire.A Psalm of David.
8.  The voice of the LORD shakes the wilderness;the LORD shakes the Wilderness of Kadesh.A Psalm of David.
9.  The voice of the LORD twists the oaksand strips the forests bare.And in His temple all cry, “Glory!” A Psalm of David.
10. The LORD sits enthroned over the flood;the LORD is enthroned as King forever.A Psalm of David.
11. The LORD gives His people strength;the LORD blesses His people with peace.
