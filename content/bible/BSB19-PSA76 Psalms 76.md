1.  God is known in Judah;His name is great in Israel.For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
2.  His tent is in Salem,His dwelling place in Zion.For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
3.  There He shattered the flaming arrows,the shield and sword and weapons of war.SelahFor the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
4.  You are resplendent with light,more majestic than mountains filled with game.For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
5.  The valiant lie plundered; they sleep their last sleep.No men of might could lift a hand.For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
6.  At Your rebuke, O God of Jacob,both horse and rider lie stunned. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
7.  You alone are to be feared.When You are angry, who can stand before You?For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
8.  From heaven You pronounced judgment,and the earth feared and was stillFor the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
9.  when God rose up to judge,to save all the lowly of the earth.SelahFor the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
10. Even the wrath of man shall praise You;with the survivors of wrath You will clothe Yourself. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
11. Make and fulfill your vows to the LORD your God;let all the neighboring lands bring tributeto Him who is to be feared.For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
12. He breaks the spirits of princes;He is feared by the kings of the earth.
