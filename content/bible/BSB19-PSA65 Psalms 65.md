1.  Praise awaits You, O God, in Zion;to You our vows will be fulfilled.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
2.  O You who listen to prayer,all people will come to You.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
3.  When iniquities prevail against me,You atone for our transgressions.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
4.  Blessed is the one You chooseand bring near to dwell in Your courts!We are filled with the goodness of Your house,the holiness of Your temple. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
5.  With awesome deeds of righteousness You answer us,O God of our salvation,the hope of all the ends of the earthand of the farthest seas.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
6.  You formed the mountains by Your power,having girded Yourself with might.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
7.  You stilled the roaring of the seas,the pounding of their waves,and the tumult of the nations.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
8.  Those who live far away fear Your wonders;You make the dawn and sunset shout for joy. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
9.  You attend to the earth and water it;with abundance You enrich it.The streams of God are full of water,for You prepare our grainby providing for the earth.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
10. You soak its furrows and level its ridges;You soften it with showers and bless its growth.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
11. You crown the year with Your bounty,and Your paths overflow with plenty.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
12. The pastures of the wilderness overflow;the hills are robed with joy.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
13. The pastures are clothed with flocks,and the valleys are decked with grain.They shout in triumph;indeed, they sing.
