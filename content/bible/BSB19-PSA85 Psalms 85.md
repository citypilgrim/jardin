1.  You showed favor to Your land, O LORD;You restored Jacob from captivity.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
2.  You forgave the iniquity of Your people;You covered all their sin.SelahFor the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
3.  You withheld all Your fury;You turned from Your burning anger. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
4.  Restore us, O God of our salvation,and put away Your displeasure toward us.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
5.  Will You be angry with us forever?Will You draw out Your anger to all generations?For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
6.  Will You not revive us again,that Your people may rejoice in You?For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
7.  Show us Your loving devotion, O LORD,and grant us Your salvation. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
8.  I will listen to what God the LORD will say;for He will surely speak peace to His people and His saints;He will not let them return to folly.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
9.  Surely His salvation is near to those who fear Him,that His glory may dwell in our land. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
10. Loving devotion and faithfulness have joined together;righteousness and peace have kissed.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
11. Faithfulness sprouts from the earth,and righteousness looks down from heaven.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
12. The LORD will indeed provide what is good,and our land will yield its increase.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
13. Righteousness will go before Himto prepare the way for His steps.
