1.  It is good to praise the LORD,and to sing praises to Your name, O Most High,A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
2.  to proclaim Your loving devotion in the morningand Your faithfulness at nightA Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
3.  with the ten-stringed harpand the melody of the lyre. A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
4.  For You, O LORD, have made me glad by Your deeds;I sing for joy at the works of Your hands.A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
5.  How great are Your works, O LORD,how deep are Your thoughts!A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
6.  A senseless man does not know,and a fool does not understand,A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
7.  that though the wicked sprout like grass,and all evildoers flourish,they will be forever destroyed. A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
8.  But You, O LORD, are exalted forever! A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
9.  For surely Your enemies, O LORD,surely Your enemies will perish;all evildoers will be scattered.A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
10. But You have exalted my horn like that of a wild ox;with fine oil I have been anointed.A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
11. My eyes see the downfall of my enemies;my ears hear the wailing of my wicked foes. A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
12. The righteous will flourish like a palm tree,and grow like a cedar in Lebanon.A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
13. Planted in the house of the LORD,they will flourish in the courts of our God.A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
14. In old age they will still bear fruit;healthy and green they will remain,A Psalm. A song for the Sabbath day.
15. to proclaim, “The LORD is upright; He is my Rock,and in Him there is no unrighteousness.”
