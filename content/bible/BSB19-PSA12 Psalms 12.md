1.  Help, O LORD, for the godly are no more;the faithful have vanished from among men.For the choirmaster. According to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
2.  They lie to one another;they speak with flattering lips and a double heart.For the choirmaster. According to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
3.  May the LORD cut off all flattering lipsand every boastful tongue.For the choirmaster. According to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
4.  They say, “With our tongues we will prevail.We own our lips—who can be our master?” For the choirmaster. According to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
5.  “For the cause of the oppressedand for the groaning of the needy,I will now arise,” says the LORD.“I will bring safety to him who yearns.” For the choirmaster. According to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
6.  The words of the LORD are flawless,like silver refined in a furnace,like gold purified sevenfold.For the choirmaster. According to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
7.  You, O LORD, will keep us;You will forever guard us from this generation.For the choirmaster. According to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
8.  The wicked wander freely,and vileness is exalted among men.
