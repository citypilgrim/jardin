1.  I will give thanks to the LORD with all my heart;I will recount all Your wonders.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
2.  I will be glad and rejoice in You;I will sing praise to Your name, O Most High. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
3.  When my enemies retreat,they stumble and perish before You.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
4.  For You have upheld my just cause;You sit on Your throne judging righteously.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
5.  You have rebuked the nations;You have destroyed the wicked;You have erased their name forever and ever.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
6.  The enemy has come to eternal ruin,and You have uprooted their cities;the very memory of them has vanished. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
7.  But the LORD abides forever;He has established His throne for judgment.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
8.  He judges the world with justice;He governs the people with equity.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
9.  The LORD is a refuge for the oppressed,a stronghold in times of trouble.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
10. Those who know Your name trust in You,for You, O LORD, have not forsaken those who seek You. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
11. Sing praises to the LORD, who dwells in Zion;proclaim His deeds among the nations.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
12. For the Avenger of bloodshed remembers;He does not ignore the cry of the afflicted. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
13. Be merciful to me, O LORD;see how my enemies afflict me!Lift me up from the gates of death,For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
14. that I may declare all Your praises—that within the gates of Daughter ZionI may rejoice in Your salvation. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
15. The nations have fallen into a pit of their making;their feet are caught in the net they have hidden.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
16. The LORD is known by the justice He brings;the wicked are ensnared by the work of their hands.Higgaion SelahFor the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
17. The wicked will return to Sheol—all the nations who forget God.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
18. For the needy will not always be forgotten;nor the hope of the oppressed forever dashed. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
19. Rise up, O LORD, do not let man prevail;let the nations be judged in Your presence.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Death of the Son.” A Psalm of David.
20. Lay terror upon them, O LORD;let the nations know they are but men.Selah
