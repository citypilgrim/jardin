1.  Have mercy on me, O God, have mercy,for in You my soul takes refuge.In the shadow of Your wings I will take shelteruntil the danger has passed. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when he fled from Saul into the cave.
2.  I cry out to God Most High,to God who fulfills His purpose for me. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when he fled from Saul into the cave.
3.  He reaches down from heaven and saves me;He rebukes those who trample me.SelahGod sends forthHis loving devotion and His truth. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when he fled from Saul into the cave.
4.  My soul is among the lions;I lie down with ravenous beasts—with men whose teeth are spears and arrows,whose tongues are sharp swords.  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when he fled from Saul into the cave.
5.  Be exalted, O God, above the heavens;may Your glory cover all the earth.  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when he fled from Saul into the cave.
6.  They spread a net for my feet;my soul was despondent.They dug a pit before me,but they themselves have fallen into it!Selah For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when he fled from Saul into the cave.
7.  My heart is steadfast, O God, my heart is steadfast.I will sing and make music. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when he fled from Saul into the cave.
8.  Awake, my glory!Awake, O harp and lyre!I will awaken the dawn.  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when he fled from Saul into the cave.
9.  I will praise You, O Lord, among the nations;I will sing Your praises among the peoples. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when he fled from Saul into the cave.
10. For Your loving devotion reaches to the heavens,and Your faithfulness to the clouds. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when he fled from Saul into the cave.
11. Be exalted, O God, above the heavens;may Your glory cover all the earth.
