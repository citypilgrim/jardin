1.  God is our refuge and strength,an ever-present help in times of trouble. For the choirmaster. Of the sons of Korah. According to Alamoth. A song.
2.  Therefore we will not fear,though the earth is transformedand the mountains are toppledinto the depths of the seas, For the choirmaster. Of the sons of Korah. According to Alamoth. A song.
3.  though their waters roar and foamand the mountains quake in the surge.Selah For the choirmaster. Of the sons of Korah. According to Alamoth. A song.
4.  There is a river whose streams delight the city of God,the holy place where the Most High dwells. For the choirmaster. Of the sons of Korah. According to Alamoth. A song.
5.  God is within her; she will not be moved.God will help her when morning dawns. For the choirmaster. Of the sons of Korah. According to Alamoth. A song.
6.  Nations rage, kingdoms crumble;the earth melts when He lifts His voice. For the choirmaster. Of the sons of Korah. According to Alamoth. A song.
7.  The LORD of Hosts is with us;the God of Jacob is our fortress.Selah For the choirmaster. Of the sons of Korah. According to Alamoth. A song.
8.  Come, see the works of the LORD,who brings devastation upon the earth. For the choirmaster. Of the sons of Korah. According to Alamoth. A song.
9.  He makes wars to cease throughout the earth;He breaks the bow and shatters the spear;He burns the shields in the fire.  For the choirmaster. Of the sons of Korah. According to Alamoth. A song.
10. “Be still and know that I am God;I will be exalted among the nations,I will be exalted over the earth.”  For the choirmaster. Of the sons of Korah. According to Alamoth. A song.
11. The LORD of Hosts is with us;the God of Jacob is our fortress.Selah
