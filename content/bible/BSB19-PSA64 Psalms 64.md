1.  Hear, O God, my voice of complaint;preserve my life from dread of the enemy. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
2.  Hide me from the scheming of the wicked,from the mob of workers of iniquity, For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
3.  who sharpen their tongues like swordsand aim their bitter words like arrows, For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
4.  ambushing the innocent in seclusion,shooting suddenly, without fear.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
5.  They hold fast to their evil purpose;they speak of hiding their snares.“Who will see them?” they say. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
6.  They devise injustice and say,“We have perfected a secret plan.”For the inner man and the heart are mysterious.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
7.  But God will shoot them with arrows;suddenly they will be wounded. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
8.  They will be made to stumble,their own tongues turned against them.All who see will shake their heads. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
9.  Then all mankind will fearand proclaim the work of God;so they will ponder what He has done.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
10. Let the righteous rejoice in the LORDand take refuge in Him;let all the upright in heart exult.
