1.  Make a joyful noise to God,all the earth! For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
2.  Sing the glory of His name;make His praise glorious. For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
3.  Say to God, “How awesome are Your deeds!So great is Your powerthat Your enemies cower before You. For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
4.  All the earth bows down to You;they sing praise to You;they sing praise to Your name.”Selah For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
5.  Come and see the works of God;how awesome are His deeds toward mankind. For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
6.  He turned the sea into dry land;they passed through the waters on foot;there we rejoiced in Him. For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
7.  He rules forever by His power;His eyes watch the nations.Do not let the rebellious exalt themselves.Selah For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
8.  Bless our God, O peoples;let the sound of His praise be heard. For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
9.  He preserves our livesand keeps our feet from slipping.  For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
10. For You, O God, have tested us;You have refined us like silver. For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
11. You led us into the net;You laid burdens on our backs. For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
12. You let men ride over our heads;we went through fire and water,but You brought us into abundance.  For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
13. I will enter Your house with burnt offerings;I will fulfill my vows to You— For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
14. the vows that my lips promisedand my mouth spoke in my distress. For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
15. I will offer You fatlings as burnt offerings,with the fragrant smoke of rams;I will offer bulls and goats.Selah For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
16. Come and listen, all you who fear God,and I will declare what He has done for me. For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
17. I cried out to Him with my mouthand praised Him with my tongue. For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
18. If I had cherished iniquity in my heart,the Lord would not have listened. For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
19. But God has surely heard;He has attended to the sound of my prayer.  For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.
20. Blessed be God, who has not rejected my prayeror withheld from me His loving devotion!
