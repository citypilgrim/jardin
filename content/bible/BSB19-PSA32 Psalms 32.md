1.  Blessed is he whose transgressions are forgiven,whose sins are covered. Of David. A Maskil.
2.  Blessed is the manwhose iniquity the LORD does not count against him,in whose spirit there is no deceit.  Of David. A Maskil.
3.  When I kept silent, my bones became brittlefrom my groaning all day long. Of David. A Maskil.
4.  For day and nightYour hand was heavy upon me;my strength was drainedas in the summer heat.Selah Of David. A Maskil.
5.  Then I acknowledged my sin to Youand did not hide my iniquity.I said, “I will confess my transgressions to the LORD,”and You forgave the guilt of my sin.Selah Of David. A Maskil.
6.  Therefore let all the godly pray to Youwhile You may be found.Surely when great waters rise,they will not come near. Of David. A Maskil.
7.  You are my hiding place.You protect me from trouble;You surround me with songs of deliverance.Selah Of David. A Maskil.
8.  I will instruct you and teach you the way you should go;I will give you counsel and watch over you. Of David. A Maskil.
9.  Do not be like the horse or mule,which have no understanding;they must be controlled with bit and bridleto make them come to you. Of David. A Maskil.
10. Many are the sorrows of the wicked,but loving devotion surrounds him who trusts in the LORD.  Of David. A Maskil.
11. Be glad in the LORD and rejoice, O righteous ones;shout for joy, all you upright in heart.
