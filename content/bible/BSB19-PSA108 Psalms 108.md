1.  My heart is steadfast, O God;I will sing and make music with all my being. A song. A Psalm of David.
2.  Awake, O harp and lyre!I will awaken the dawn. A song. A Psalm of David.
3.  I will praise You, O LORD, among the nations;I will sing Your praises among the peoples. A song. A Psalm of David.
4.  For Your loving devotion extends beyond the heavens,and Your faithfulness reaches to the clouds. A song. A Psalm of David.
5.  Be exalted, O God, above the heavens;may Your glory cover all the earth. A song. A Psalm of David.
6.  Respond and save us with Your right hand,that Your beloved may be delivered.  A song. A Psalm of David.
7.  God has spoken from His sanctuary:“I will triumph!I will parcel out Shechemand apportion the Valley of Succoth. A song. A Psalm of David.
8.  Gilead is Mine, and Manasseh is Mine;Ephraim is My helmet, Judah is My scepter. A song. A Psalm of David.
9.  Moab is My washbasin;upon Edom I toss My sandal;over Philistia I shout in triumph.”  A song. A Psalm of David.
10. Who will bring me to the fortified city?Who will lead me to Edom? A song. A Psalm of David.
11. Have You not rejected us, O God?Will You no longer march out, O God, with our armies? A song. A Psalm of David.
12. Give us aid against the enemy,for the help of man is worthless. A song. A Psalm of David.
13. With God we will perform with valor,and He will trample our enemies.
