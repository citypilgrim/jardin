1.  I waited patiently for the LORD;He inclined to me and heard my cry. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
2.  He lifted me up from the pit of despair,out of the miry clay;He set my feet upon a rock,and made my footsteps firm. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
3.  He put a new song in my mouth,a hymn of praise to our God.Many will see and fearand put their trust in the LORD.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
4.  Blessed is the manwho has made the LORD his trust,who has not turned to the proud,nor to those who lapse into falsehood. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
5.  Many, O LORD my God,are the wonders You have done,and the plans You have for us—none can compare to You—if I proclaim and declare them,they are more than I can count.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
6.  Sacrifice and offering You did not desire,but my ears You have opened.Burnt offerings and sin offeringsYou did not require. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
7.  Then I said, “Here I am, I have come—it is written about me in the scroll: For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
8.  I delight to do Your will, O my God;Your law is within my heart.”  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
9.  I proclaim righteousness in the great assembly;behold, I do not seal my lips,as You, O LORD, do know. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
10. I have not covered up Your righteousness in my heart;I have declared Your faithfulness and salvation;I have not concealed Your loving devotion and faithfulnessfrom the great assembly.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
11. O LORD, do not withhold Your mercy from me;Your loving devotion and faithfulness will always guard me. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
12. For evils without number surround me;my sins have overtaken me, so that I cannot see.They are more than the hairs of my head,and my heart has failed within me. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
13. Be pleased, O LORD, to deliver me;hurry, O LORD, to help me.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
14. May those who seek my lifebe ashamed and confounded;may those who wish me harmbe repelled and humiliated. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
15. May those who say to me, “Aha, aha!”be appalled at their own shame. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
16. May all who seek Yourejoice and be glad in You;may those who love Your salvationalways say, “The LORD be magnified!”  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
17. But I am poor and needy;may the Lord think of me.You are my helper and deliverer;O my God, do not delay.
