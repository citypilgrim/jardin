8.  But I am like an olive treeflourishing in the house of God;I trust in the loving devotion of Godforever and ever. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of David. After Doeg the Edomite went to Saul and told him, “David has gone to the house of Ahimelech.”
9.  I will praise You forever,because You have done it.I will wait on Your name—for it is good—in the presence of Your saints.
