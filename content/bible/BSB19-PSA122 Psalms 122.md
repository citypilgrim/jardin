1.  I was glad when they said to me,“Let us go to the house of the LORD.”A song of ascents. Of David.
2.  Our feet are standing in your gates,O Jerusalem. A song of ascents. Of David.
3.  Jerusalem is built upas a city united together,A song of ascents. Of David.
4.  where the tribes go up,the tribes of the LORD,as a testimony for Israel,to give thanks to the name of the LORD.A song of ascents. Of David.
5.  For there the thrones of judgment stand,the thrones of the house of David. A song of ascents. Of David.
6.  Pray for the peace of Jerusalem:“May those who love you prosper.A song of ascents. Of David.
7.  May there be peace within your walls,and prosperity inside your fortresses.”A song of ascents. Of David.
8.  For the sake of my brothers and friends,I will say, “Peace be within you.”A song of ascents. Of David.
9.  For the sake of the house of the LORD our God,I will seek your prosperity.
