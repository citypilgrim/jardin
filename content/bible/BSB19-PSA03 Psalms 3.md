1.  O LORD, how my foes have increased!How many rise up against me! A Psalm of David, when he fled from his son Absalom.
2.  Many say of me,“God will not deliver him.”Selah A Psalm of David, when he fled from his son Absalom.
3.  But You, O LORD, are a shield around me,my glory, and the One who lifts my head. A Psalm of David, when he fled from his son Absalom.
4.  To the LORD I cry aloud,and He answers me from His holy mountain.Selah A Psalm of David, when he fled from his son Absalom.
5.  I lie down and sleep;I wake again, for the LORD sustains me. A Psalm of David, when he fled from his son Absalom.
6.  I will not fear the myriadsset against me on every side.  A Psalm of David, when he fled from his son Absalom.
7.  Arise, O LORD!Save me, O my God!Strike all my enemies on the jaw;break the teeth of the wicked. A Psalm of David, when he fled from his son Absalom.
8.  Salvation belongs to the LORD;may Your blessing be on Your people.Selah
