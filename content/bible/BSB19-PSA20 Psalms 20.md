1.  May the LORD answer you in the day of trouble;may the name of the God of Jacob protect you.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
2.  May He send you help from the sanctuaryand sustain you from Zion.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
3.  May He remember all your giftsand look favorably on your burnt offerings.SelahFor the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
4.  May He give you the desires of your heartand make all your plans succeed.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
5.  May we shout for joy at your victoryand raise a banner in the name of our God.May the LORD grant all your petitions. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
6.  Now I know that the LORD saves His anointed;He answers him from His holy heavenwith the saving power of His right hand.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
7.  Some trust in chariots and others in horses,but we trust in the name of the LORD our God.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
8.  They collapse and fall,but we rise up and stand firm.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
9.  O LORD, save the king.Answer us on the day we call.
