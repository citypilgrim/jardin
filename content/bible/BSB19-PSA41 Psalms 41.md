1.  Blessed is the one who cares for the poor;the LORD will deliver him in the day of trouble. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
2.  The LORD will protect and preserve him;He will bless him in the landand refuse to surrender himto the will of his foes. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
3.  The LORD will sustain him on his bed of illnessand restore him from his bed of sickness.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
4.  I said, “O LORD, be gracious to me;heal me, for I have sinned against You.” For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
5.  My enemies say with malice:“When will he die and be forgotten?” For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
6.  My visitor speaks falsehood;he gathers slander in his heart;he goes out and spreads it abroad. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
7.  All who hate me whisper against me;they imagine the worst for me: For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
8.  “A vile disease has been poured into him;he will never get up from where he lies!” For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
9.  Even my close friend whom I trusted,the one who shared my bread,has lifted up his heel against me.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
10. But You, O LORD, be gracious to me and raise me up,that I may repay them. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
11. By this I know that You delight in me,for my enemy does not triumph over me. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
12. In my integrity You uphold meand set me in Your presence forever.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
13. Blessed be the LORD, the God of Israel,from everlasting to everlasting. Amen and Amen.
