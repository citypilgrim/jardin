1.  O LORD, You have searched meand known me.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
2.  You know when I sit and when I rise;You understand my thoughts from afar.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
3.  You search out my path and my lying down;You are aware of all my ways.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
4.  Even before a word is on my tongue,You know all about it, O LORD.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
5.  You hem me in behind and before;You have laid Your hand upon me.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
6.  Such knowledge is too wonderful for me,too lofty for me to attain. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
7.  Where can I go to escape Your Spirit?Where can I flee from Your presence?For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
8.  If I ascend to the heavens, You are there;if I make my bed in Sheol, You are there.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
9.  If I rise on the wings of the dawn,if I settle by the farthest sea,For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
10. even there Your hand will guide me;Your right hand will hold me fast.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
11. If I say, “Surely the darkness will hide me,and the light become night around me”—For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
12. even the darkness is not dark to You,but the night shines like the day,for darkness is as light to You. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
13. For You formed my inmost being;You knit me together in my mother’s womb.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
14. I praise You,for I am fearfully and wonderfully made.Marvelous are Your works,and I know this very well.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
15. My frame was not hidden from Youwhen I was made in secret,when I was woven togetherin the depths of the earth.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
16. Your eyes saw my unformed body;all my days were written in Your bookand ordained for mebefore one of them came to be. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
17. How precious to me are Your thoughts, O God,how vast is their sum!For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
18. If I were to count them,they would outnumber the grains of sand;and when I awake,I am still with You. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
19. O God, that You would slay the wicked—away from me, you bloodthirsty men—For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
20. who speak of You deceitfully;Your enemies take Your name in vain.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
21. Do I not hate those who hate You, O LORD,and detest those who rise against You?For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
22. I hate them with perfect hatred;I count them as my enemies. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
23. Search me, O God, and know my heart;test me and know my concerns.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
24. See if there is any offensive way in me;lead me in the way everlasting.
