1.  We have heard with our ears, O God;our fathers have told usthe work You did in their days,in the days of old. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
2.  With Your hand You drove out the nationsand planted our fathers there;You crushed the peoplesand cast them out. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
3.  For it was not by their sword that they took the land;their arm did not bring them victory.It was by Your right hand,Your arm, and the light of Your face,because You favored them.  For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
4.  You are my King, O God,who ordains victories for Jacob. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
5.  Through You we repel our foes;through Your name we trample our enemies. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
6.  For I do not trust in my bow,nor does my sword save me. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
7.  For You save us from our enemies;You put those who hate us to shame. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
8.  In God we have boasted all day long,and Your name we will praise forever.Selah For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
9.  But You have rejected and humbled us;You no longer go forth with our armies. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
10. You have made us retreat from the foe,and those who hate us have plundered us. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
11. You have given us up as sheep to be devoured;You have scattered us among the nations. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
12. You sell Your people for nothing;no profit do You gain from their sale.  For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
13. You have made us a reproach to our neighbors,a mockery and derision to those around us. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
14. You have made us a byword among the nations,a laughingstock among the peoples. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
15. All day long my disgrace is before me,and shame has covered my face, For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
16. at the voice of the scorner and reviler,because of the enemy, bent on revenge.  For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
17. All this has come upon us,though we have not forgotten Youor betrayed Your covenant. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
18. Our hearts have not turned back;our steps have not strayed from Your path. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
19. But You have crushed us in the lair of jackals;You have covered us with deepest darkness.  For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
20. If we had forgotten the name of our Godor spread out our hands to a foreign god, For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
21. would not God have discovered,since He knows the secrets of the heart? For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
22. Yet for Your sake we face death all day long;we are considered as sheep to be slaughtered.  For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
23. Wake up, O Lord! Why are You sleeping?Arise! Do not reject us forever. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
24. Why do You hide Your faceand forget our affliction and oppression? For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
25. For our soul has sunk to the dust;our bodies cling to the earth. For the choirmaster. A Maskil of the sons of Korah.
26. Rise up; be our help!Redeem us on account of Your loving devotion.
