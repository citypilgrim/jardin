1.  God arises. His enemies are scattered,and those who hate Him flee His presence.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
2.  As smoke is blown away,You will drive them out;as wax melts before the fire,the wicked will perish in the presence of God.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
3.  But the righteous will be gladand rejoice before God;they will celebrate with joy. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
4.  Sing to God!Sing praises to His name.Exalt Him who rides on the clouds—His name is the LORD—and rejoice before Him.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
5.  A father of the fatherless,and a defender of the widows,is God in His holy habitation.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
6.  God settles the lonely in families;He leads the prisoners out to prosperity,but the rebellious dwell in a sun-scorched land. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
7.  O God, when You went out before Your people,when You marched through the wasteland,SelahFor the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
8.  the earth shook and the heavens poured down rainbefore God, the One on Sinai,before God, the God of Israel.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
9.  You sent abundant rain, O God;You refreshed Your weary inheritance.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
10. Your flock settled therein;O God, from Your bounty You provided for the poor. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
11. The Lord gives the command;a great company of women proclaim it:For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
12. “Kings and their armies flee in haste;she who waits at home divides the plunder.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
13. Though you lie down among the sheepfolds,the wings of the dove are covered with silver,and her feathers with shimmering gold.”For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
14. When the Almighty scattered the kings in the land,it was like the snow falling on Zalmon. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
15. A mountain of God is Mount Bashan;a mountain of many peaks is Mount Bashan.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
16. Why do you gaze in envy, O mountains of many peaks?This is the mountain God chose for His dwelling,where the LORD will surely dwell forever. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
17. The chariots of God are tens of thousands—thousands of thousands are they;the Lord is in His sanctuaryas He was at Sinai.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
18. You have ascended on high;You have led captives away.You have received gifts from men,even from the rebellious,that the LORD God may dwell there.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
19. Blessed be the Lord,who daily bears our burden,the God of our salvation.SelahFor the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
20. Our God is a God of deliverance;the Lord GOD is our rescuer from death.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
21. Surely God will crush the heads of His enemies,the hairy crowns of those who persist in guilty ways.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
22. The Lord said, “I will retrieve them from Bashan,I will bring them up from the depths of the sea,For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
23. that your foot may be dippedin the blood of your foes—the tongues of your dogs in the same.” For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
24. They have seen Your procession, O God—the march of my God and King into the sanctuary.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
25. The singers lead the way,the musicians follow after,among the maidens playing tambourines.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
26. Bless God in the great congregation;bless the LORD from the fountain of Israel.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
27. There is Benjamin, the youngest, ruling them,the princes of Judah in their company,the princes of Zebulun and of Naphtali. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
28. Summon Your power, O God;show Your strength, O God,which You have exerted on our behalf.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
29. Because of Your temple at Jerusalemkings will bring You gifts.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
30. Rebuke the beast in the reeds,the herd of bulls among the calves of the nations,until it submits, bringing bars of silver.Scatter the nations who delight in war.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
31. Envoys will arrive from Egypt;Cush will stretch out her hands to God. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
32. Sing to God, O kingdoms of the earth;sing praises to the Lord—SelahFor the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
33. to Him who rides upon the highest heavens of old;behold, His mighty voice resounds.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
34. Ascribe the power to God,whose majesty is over Israel,whose strength is in the skies.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.
35. O God, You are awesome in Your sanctuary;the God of Israel Himselfgives strength and power to His people. Blessed be God!
