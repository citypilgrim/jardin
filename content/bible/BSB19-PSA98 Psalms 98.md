1.  Sing to the LORD a new song,for He has done wonders;His right hand and holy armhave gained Him the victory. A Psalm.
2.  The LORD has proclaimed His salvationand revealed His righteousness to the nations. A Psalm.
3.  He has remembered His love and faithfulnessto the house of Israel;all the ends of the earthhave seen the salvation of our God.  A Psalm.
4.  Make a joyful noise to the LORD, all the earth;break forth—let your cry ring out, and sing praises! A Psalm.
5.  Sing praises to the LORD with the lyre,in melodious song with the harp. A Psalm.
6.  With trumpets and the blast of the ram’s hornshout for joy before the LORD, the King.  A Psalm.
7.  Let the sea resound, and all that fills it,the world, and all who dwell in it. A Psalm.
8.  Let the rivers clap their hands,let the mountains sing together for joy A Psalm.
9.  before the LORD,for He comes to judge the earth.He will judge the world with righteousnessand the peoples with equity.
