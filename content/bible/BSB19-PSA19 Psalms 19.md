1.  The heavens declare the glory of God;the skies proclaim the work of His hands.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
2.  Day after day they pour forth speech;night after night they reveal knowledge.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
3.  Without speech or language,without a sound to be heard,For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
4.  their voice has gone out into all the earth,their words to the ends of the world. In the heavens He has pitcheda tent for the sun.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
5.  Like a bridegroom emerging from his chamber,like a champion rejoicing to run his course,For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
6.  it rises at one end of the heavensand runs its circuit to the other;nothing is deprived of its warmth. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
7.  The Law of the LORD is perfect,reviving the soul;the testimony of the LORD is trustworthy,making wise the simple.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
8.  The precepts of the LORD are right,bringing joy to the heart;the commandments of the LORD are radiant,giving light to the eyes.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
9.  The fear of the LORD is pure,enduring forever;the judgments of the LORD are true,being altogether righteous.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
10. They are more precious than gold,than much pure gold;they are sweeter than honey,than honey from the comb.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
11. By them indeed Your servant is warned;in keeping them is great reward. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
12. Who can discern his own errors?Cleanse me from my hidden faults.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
13. Keep Your servant also from willful sins;may they not rule over me.Then I will be blamelessand cleansed of great transgression.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
14. May the words of my mouthand the meditation of my heartbe pleasing in Your sight,O LORD, my Rock and my Redeemer.
