1.  Make a joyful noise to the LORD,all the earth.  A Psalm of thanksgiving.
2.  Serve the LORD with gladness;come into His presence with joyful songs. A Psalm of thanksgiving.
3.  Know that the LORD is God.It is He who made us, and we are His;we are His people, and the sheep of His pasture. A Psalm of thanksgiving.
4.  Enter His gates with thanksgivingand His courts with praise;give thanks to Him and bless His name.  A Psalm of thanksgiving.
5.  For the LORD is good,and His loving devotion endures forever;His faithfulness continues to all generations.
