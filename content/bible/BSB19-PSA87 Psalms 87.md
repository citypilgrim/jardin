1.  He has founded His cityon the holy mountains.A Psalm of the sons of Korah. A song.
2.  The LORD loves the gates of Zionmore than all the dwellings of Jacob.A Psalm of the sons of Korah. A song.
3.  Glorious things are ascribed to you,O city of God.SelahA Psalm of the sons of Korah. A song.
4.  “I will mention Rahab and Babylonamong those who know Me—along with Philistia, Tyre, and Cush—when I say, ‘This one was born in Zion.’” A Psalm of the sons of Korah. A song.
5.  And it will be said of Zion:“This one and that one were born in her,and the Most High Himself will establish her.” A Psalm of the sons of Korah. A song.
6.  The LORD will record in the register of the peoples:“This one was born in Zion.”SelahA Psalm of the sons of Korah. A song.
7.  Singers and pipers will proclaim,“All my springs of joy are in You.”
