1.  I will bless the LORD at all times;His praise will always be on my lips. Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
2.  My soul boasts in the LORD;let the oppressed hear and rejoice. Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
3.  Magnify the LORD with me;let us exalt His name together.  Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
4.  I sought the LORD, and He answered me;He delivered me from all my fears. Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
5.  Those who look to Him are radiant with joy;their faces shall never be ashamed. Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
6.  This poor man called out, and the LORD heard him;He saved him from all his troubles. Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
7.  The angel of the LORD encamps around those who fear Him,and he delivers them.  Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
8.  Taste and see that the LORD is good;blessed is the man who takes refuge in Him! Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
9.  Fear the LORD, you His saints,for those who fear Him lack nothing. Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
10. Young lions go lacking and hungry,but those who seek the LORD lack no good thing. Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
11. Come, children, listen to me;I will teach you the fear of the LORD.  Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
12. Who is the man who delights in life,who desires to see good days? Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
13. Keep your tongue from eviland your lips from deceitful speech. Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
14. Turn away from evil and do good;seek peace and pursue it. Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
15. The eyes of the LORD are on the righteous,and His ears are inclined to their cry. Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
16. But the face of the LORD is against those who do evil,to wipe out all memory of them from the earth.  Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
17. The righteous cry out, and the LORD hears;He delivers them from all their troubles. Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
18. The LORD is near to the brokenhearted;He saves the contrite in spirit.  Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
19. Many are the afflictions of the righteous,but the LORD delivers him from them all. Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
20. He protects all his bones;not one of them will be broken.  Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
21. Evil will slay the wicked,and the haters of the righteous will be condemned. Of David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, so that the king drove him away.
22. The LORD redeems His servants,and none who take refuge in Him will be condemned.
