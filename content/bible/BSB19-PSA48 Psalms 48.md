1.  Great is the LORD,and greatly to be praisedin the city of our God,His holy mountain.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
2.  Beautiful in loftiness,the joy of all the earth,like the peaks of Zaphon is Mount Zion,the city of the great King.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
3.  God is in her citadels;He has shown Himself to be a fortress. A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
4.  For behold, the kings assembled;they all advanced together.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
5.  They saw and were astounded;they fled in terror.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
6.  Trembling seized them there,anguish like a woman in labor.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
7.  With a wind from the eastYou wrecked the ships of Tarshish. A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
8.  As we have heard, so we have seenin the city of the LORD of Hosts,in the city of our God:God will establish her forever.SelahA song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
9.  Within Your temple, O God,we contemplate Your loving devotion.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
10. Your name, O God, like Your praise,reaches to the ends of the earth;Your right hand is full of righteousness.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
11. Mount Zion is glad,the daughters of Judah rejoice,on account of Your judgments. A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
12. March around Zion, encircle her,count her towers,A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
13. consider her ramparts, tour her citadels,that you may tell the next generation.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
14. For this God is our God forever and ever;He will be our guide even till death.
