1.  In You, O LORD, I have taken refuge;let me never be put to shame;save me by Your righteousness. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
2.  Incline Your ear to me;come quickly to my rescue.Be my rock of refuge,the stronghold of my deliverance.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
3.  For You are my rock and my fortress;lead me and guide me for the sake of Your name. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
4.  You free me from the net laid out for me,for You are my refuge. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
5.  Into Your hands I commit my spirit;You have redeemed me, O LORD, God of truth.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
6.  I hate those who cling to worthless idols,but in the LORD I trust. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
7.  I will be glad and rejoice in Your loving devotion,for You have seen my affliction;You have known the anguish of my soul. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
8.  You have not delivered me to the enemy;You have set my feet in the open.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
9.  Be merciful to me, O LORD,for I am in distress;my eyes fail from sorrow,my soul and body as well. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
10. For my life is consumed with griefand my years with groaning;my iniquity has drained my strength,and my bones are wasting away. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
11. Among all my enemies I am a disgrace,and among my neighbors even more.I am dreaded by my friends—they flee when they see me on the street. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
12. I am forgotten like a dead man, out of mind.I am like a broken vessel. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
13. For I hear the slander of many;there is terror on every side.They conspire against meand plot to take my life.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
14. But I trust in You, O LORD;I say, “You are my God.” For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
15. My times are in Your hands;deliver me from my enemiesand from those who pursue me. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
16. Make Your face shine on Your servant;save me by Your loving devotion.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
17. O LORD, let me not be ashamed,for I have called on You.Let the wicked be put to shame;let them lie silent in Sheol. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
18. May lying lips be silenced—lips that speak with arrogance against the righteous,full of pride and contempt.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
19. How great is Your goodnesswhich You have laid up for those who fear You,which You have bestowed before the sons of menon those who take refuge in You! For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
20. You hide them in the secret place of Your presencefrom the schemes of men.You conceal them in Your shelterfrom accusing tongues.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
21. Blessed be the LORD,for He has shown me His loving devotionin a city under siege. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
22. In my alarm I said,“I am cut off from Your sight!”But You heard my plea for mercywhen I called to You for help.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
23. Love the LORD, all His saints.The LORD preserves the faithful,but fully repays the arrogant. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
24. Be strong and courageous,all you who hope in the LORD.
