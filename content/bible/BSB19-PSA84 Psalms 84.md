1.  How lovely is Your dwelling place,O LORD of Hosts! For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
2.  My soul longs, even faints,for the courts of the LORD;my heart and my flesh cry outfor the living God.  For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
3.  Even the sparrow has found a home,and the swallow a nest for herself,where she places her young near Your altars,O LORD of Hosts, my King and my God. For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
4.  How blessed are those who dwell in Your house!They are ever praising You.Selah For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
5.  Blessed are those whose strength is in You,whose hearts are set on pilgrimage. For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
6.  As they pass through the Valley of Baca,they make it a place of springs;even the autumn rain covers it with pools. For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
7.  They go from strength to strength,until each appears before God in Zion.  For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
8.  O LORD God of Hosts, hear my prayer;give ear, O God of Jacob.Selah For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
9.  Take notice of our shield, O God,and look with favor on the face of Your anointed. For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
10. For better is one day in Your courtsthan a thousand elsewhere.I would rather be a doorkeeper in the house of my Godthan dwell in the tents of the wicked. For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
11. For the LORD God is a sun and a shield;the LORD gives grace and glory;He withholds no good thingfrom those who walk with integrity.  For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
12. O LORD of Hosts,how blessed is the man who trusts in You!
