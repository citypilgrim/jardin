1.  O LORD, do not rebuke me in Your angeror discipline me in Your wrath. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments, according to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
2.  Be merciful to me, O LORD, for I am frail;heal me, O LORD, for my bones are in agony. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments, according to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
3.  My soul is deeply distressed.How long, O LORD, how long?  For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments, according to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
4.  Turn, O LORD, and deliver my soul;save me because of Your loving devotion. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments, according to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
5.  For there is no mention of You in death;who can praise You from Sheol?  For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments, according to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
6.  I am weary from groaning;all night I flood my bed with weepingand drench my couch with tears. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments, according to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
7.  My eyes fail from grief;they grow dim because of all my foes.  For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments, according to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
8.  Depart from me, all you workers of iniquity,for the LORD has heard my weeping. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments, according to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
9.  The LORD has heard my cry for mercy;the LORD accepts my prayer. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments, according to Sheminith. A Psalm of David.
10. All my enemies will be ashamed and dismayed;they will turn back in sudden disgrace.
