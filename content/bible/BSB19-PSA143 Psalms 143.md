1.  O LORD, hear my prayer.In Your faithfulness, give ear to my plea;in Your righteousness, answer me.A Psalm of David.
2.  Do not bring Your servant into judgment,for no one alive is righteous before You. A Psalm of David.
3.  For the enemy has pursued my soul,crushing my life to the ground,making me dwell in darknesslike those long since dead.A Psalm of David.
4.  My spirit grows faint within me;my heart is dismayed inside me. A Psalm of David.
5.  I remember the days of old;I meditate on all Your works;I consider the work of Your hands.A Psalm of David.
6.  I stretch out my hands to You;my soul thirsts for You like a parched land.SelahA Psalm of David.
7.  Answer me quickly, O LORD;my spirit fails.Do not hide Your face from me,or I will be like those who descend to the Pit.A Psalm of David.
8.  Let me hear Your loving devotion in the morning,for I have put my trust in You.Teach me the way I should walk,for to You I lift up my soul.A Psalm of David.
9.  Deliver me from my enemies, O LORD;I flee to You for refuge.A Psalm of David.
10. Teach me to do Your will,for You are my God.May Your good Spirit lead meon level ground. A Psalm of David.
11. For the sake of Your name, O LORD,revive me.In Your righteousness,bring my soul out of trouble.A Psalm of David.
12. And in Your loving devotion,cut off my enemies.Destroy all who afflict me,for I am Your servant.
