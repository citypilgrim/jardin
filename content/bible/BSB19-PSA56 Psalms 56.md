8.  You have taken account of my wanderings.Put my tears in Your bottle—are they not in Your book? For the choirmaster. To the tune of “A Dove on Distant Oaks.” A Miktam of David, when the Philistines seized him in Gath.

11. in God I trust; I will not be afraid.What can man do to me?  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “A Dove on Distant Oaks.” A Miktam of David, when the Philistines seized him in Gath.
12. Your vows are upon me, O God;I will render thank offerings to You. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “A Dove on Distant Oaks.” A Miktam of David, when the Philistines seized him in Gath.
13. For You have delivered my soul from death,and my feet from stumbling,that I may walk before Godin the light of life.
