1.  Hear, O LORD, my righteous plea;listen to my cry.Give ear to my prayer—it comes from lips free of deceit.A prayer of David.
2.  May my vindication come from Your presence;may Your eyes see what is right. A prayer of David.
3.  You have tried my heart;You have visited me in the night.You have tested me and found no evil;I have resolved not to sin with my mouth.A prayer of David.
4.  As for the deeds of men—by the word of Your lipsI have avoided the ways of the violent.A prayer of David.
5.  My steps have held to Your paths;my feet have not slipped. A prayer of David.
6.  I call on You, O God,for You will answer me.Incline Your ear to me;hear my words.A prayer of David.
7.  Show the wonders of Your loving devotion,You who save by Your right handthose who seek refuge from their foes.A prayer of David.
8.  Keep me as the apple of Your eye;hide me in the shadow of Your wingsA prayer of David.
9.  from the wicked who assail me,from my mortal enemies who surround me. A prayer of David.
10. They have closed their callous hearts;their mouths speak with arrogance.A prayer of David.
11. They have tracked us down, and now surround us;their eyes are set to cast us to the ground,A prayer of David.
12. like a lion greedy for prey,like a young lion lurking in ambush. A prayer of David.
13. Arise, O LORD, confront them!Bring them to their knees;deliver me from the wicked by Your sword,A prayer of David.
14. from such men, O LORD, by Your hand—from men of the worldwhose portion is in this life.May You fill the bellies of Your treasured ones and satisfy their sons,so they leave their abundance to their children. A prayer of David.
15. As for me, I will behold Your face in righteousness;when I awake, I will be satisfied in Your presence.
