1.  Hear this, all you peoples;listen, all inhabitants of the world, For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
2.  both low and high,rich and poor alike. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
3.  My mouth will impart wisdom,and the meditation of my heart will bring understanding. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
4.  I will incline my ear to a proverb;I will express my riddle with the harp:  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
5.  Why should I fear in times of trouble,when wicked usurpers surround me? For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
6.  They trust in their wealthand boast in their great riches. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
7.  No man can possibly redeem his brotheror pay his ransom to God. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
8.  For the redemption of his soul is costly,and never can payment suffice, For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
9.  that he should live on foreverand not see decay.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
10. For it is clear that wise men die,and the foolish and the senseless both perishand leave their wealth to others. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
11. Their graves are their eternal homes—their dwellings for endless generations—even though their lands were their namesakes. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
12. But a man, despite his wealth, cannot endure;he is like the beasts that perish.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
13. This is the fate of the self-confidentand their followers who endorse their sayings.Selah For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
14. Like sheep they are destined for Sheol.Death will be their shepherd.The upright will rule them in the morning,and their form will decay in Sheol,far from their lofty abode. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
15. But God will redeem my life from Sheol,for He will surely take me to Himself.Selah For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
16. Do not be amazed when a man grows rich,when the splendor of his house increases. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
17. For when he dies, he will carry nothing away;his abundance will not follow him down. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
18. Though in his lifetime he blesses his soul—and men praise you when you prosper— For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
19. he will join the generation of his fathers,who will never see the light of day. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
20. A man who has riches without understandingis like the beasts that perish.
