1.  Deliver me from my enemies, O my God;protect me from those who rise against me. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
2.  Deliver me from workers of iniquity,and save me from men of bloodshed. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
3.  See how they lie in wait for me.Fierce men conspire against mefor no transgression or sin of my own, O LORD. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
4.  For no fault of my own,they move swiftly to attack me.Arise to help me, and take notice. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
5.  O LORD God of Hosts, the God of Israel,rouse Yourself to punish all the nations;show no mercy to the wicked traitors.Selah For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
6.  They return in the evening, snarling like dogsand prowling around the city. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
7.  See what they spew from their mouths—sharp words from their lips:“For who can hear us?” For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
8.  But You, O LORD, laugh at them;You scoff at all the nations.  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
9.  I will keep watch for You, O my strength,because You, O God, are my fortress. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
10. My God of loving devotion will come to meet me;God will let me stare down my foes.  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
11. Do not kill them,or my people will forget.Scatter them by Your power,and bring them down,O Lord, our shield. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
12. By the sins of their mouthsand the words of their lips,let them be trapped in their pride,in the curses and lies they utter. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
13. Consume them in wrath;consume them till they are no more,so it may be known to the ends of the earththat God rules over Jacob.Selah For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
14. They return in the evening,snarling like dogsand prowling around the city. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
15. They scavenge for food,and growl if they are not satisfied.  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
16. But I will sing of Your strengthand proclaim Your loving devotion in the morning.For You are my fortress,my refuge in times of trouble. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Miktam of David, when Saul sent men to watch David’s house in order to kill him.
17. To You, O my strength, I sing praises,for You, O God, are my fortress,my God of loving devotion.
