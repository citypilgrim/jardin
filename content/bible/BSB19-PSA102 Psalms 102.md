1.  Hear my prayer, O LORD;let my cry for help come before You.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
2.  Do not hide Your face from mein my day of distress.Incline Your ear to me;answer me quickly when I call. A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
3.  For my days vanish like smoke,and my bones burn like glowing embers.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
4.  My heart is afflicted, and withered like grass;I even forget to eat my bread.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
5.  Through my loud groaningmy flesh clings to my bones.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
6.  I am like a desert owl,like an owl among the ruins.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
7.  I lie awake;I am like a lone bird on a housetop. A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
8.  All day long my enemies taunt me;they ridicule me and curse me.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
9.  For I have eaten ashes like breadand mixed my drink with tearsA prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
10. because of Your indignation and wrath,for You have picked me up and cast me aside.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
11. My days are like lengthening shadows,and I wither away like grass. A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
12. But You, O LORD, sit enthroned forever;Your renown endures to all generations.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
13. You will rise up and have compassion on Zion,for it is time to show her favor—the appointed time has come.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
14. For Your servants delight in her stonesand take pity on her dust. A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
15. So the nations will fear the name of the LORD,and all the kings of the earth will fear Your glory.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
16. For the LORD will rebuild Zion;He has appeared in His glory.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
17. He will turn toward the prayer of the destitute;He will not despise their prayer. A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
18. Let this be written for the generation to come,so that a people not yet created may praise the LORD.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
19. For He looked down from the heights of His sanctuary;the LORD gazed out from heaven to earthA prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
20. to hear a prisoner’s groaning,to release those condemned to death,A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
21. that they may proclaim the name of the LORD in Zionand praise Him in Jerusalem,A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
22. when peoples and kingdoms assembleto serve the LORD. A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
23. He has broken my strength on the way;He has cut short my days.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
24. I say: “O my God, do not take me in the midst of my days!Your years go on through all generations.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
25. In the beginning You laid the foundations of the earth,and the heavens are the work of Your hands.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
26. They will perish, but You remain;they will all wear out like a garment.Like clothing You will change them,and they will be passed on.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
27. But You remain the same,and Your years will never end.A prayer of one who is afflicted, when he grows faint and pours out his lament before the LORD.
28. The children of Your servants will dwell securely,and their descendants will be established before You.”
