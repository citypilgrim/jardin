1.  Hear us, O Shepherd of Israel,who leads Joseph like a flock;You who sit enthroned between the cherubim,shine forthFor the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
2.  before Ephraim, Benjamin, and Manasseh.Rally Your mighty powerand come to save us.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
3.  Restore us, O God,and cause Your face to shine upon us,that we may be saved. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
4.  O LORD God of Hosts,how long will Your anger smolderagainst the prayers of Your people?For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
5.  You fed them with the bread of tearsand made them drink the full measure of their tears.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
6.  You make us contend with our neighbors;our enemies mock us.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
7.  Restore us, O God of Hosts,and cause Your face to shine upon us,that we may be saved. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
8.  You uprooted a vine from Egypt;You drove out the nations and transplanted it.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
9.  You cleared the ground for it,and it took root and filled the land.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
10. The mountains were covered by its shade,and the mighty cedars with its branches.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
11. It sent out its branches to the Sea,and its shoots toward the River. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
12. Why have You broken down its walls,so that all who pass by pick its fruit?For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
13. The boar from the forest ravages it,and the creatures of the field feed upon it.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
14. Return, O God of Hosts, we pray!Look down from heaven and see!Attend to this vine—For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
15. the root Your right hand has planted,the son You have raised up for Yourself. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
16. Your vine has been cut down and burned;they perish at the rebuke of Your countenance.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
17. Let Your hand be upon the man at Your right hand,on the son of man You have raised up for Yourself.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
18. Then we will not turn away from You;revive us, and we will call on Your name.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Lilies of the Covenant.” A Psalm of Asaph.
19. Restore us, O LORD God of Hosts;cause Your face to shine upon us,that we may be saved.
