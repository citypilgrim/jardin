1.  I will exalt You, O LORD,for You have lifted me upand have not allowed my foesto rejoice over me.A Psalm. A song for the dedication of the temple. Of David.
2.  O LORD my God, I cried to You for help,and You healed me.A Psalm. A song for the dedication of the temple. Of David.
3.  O LORD, You pulled me up from Sheol;You spared me from descending into the Pit. A Psalm. A song for the dedication of the temple. Of David.
4.  Sing to the LORD, O you His saints,and praise His holy name.A Psalm. A song for the dedication of the temple. Of David.
5.  For His anger is fleeting,but His favor lasts a lifetime.Weeping may stay the night,but joy comes in the morning. A Psalm. A song for the dedication of the temple. Of David.
6.  In prosperity I said,“I will never be shaken.”A Psalm. A song for the dedication of the temple. Of David.
7.  O LORD, You favored me;You made my mountain stand strong. When You hid Your face,I was dismayed.A Psalm. A song for the dedication of the temple. Of David.
8.  To You, O LORD, I called,and I begged my Lord for mercy:A Psalm. A song for the dedication of the temple. Of David.
9.  “What gain is there in my bloodshed,in my descent to the Pit?Will the dust praise You?Will it proclaim Your faithfulness?A Psalm. A song for the dedication of the temple. Of David.
10. Hear me, O LORD, and have mercy;O LORD, be my helper.” A Psalm. A song for the dedication of the temple. Of David.
11. You turned my mourning into dancing;You peeled off my sackcloth and clothed me with joy,A Psalm. A song for the dedication of the temple. Of David.
12. that my heart may sing Your praises and not be silent.O LORD my God, I will give thanks forever.
