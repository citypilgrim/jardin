1.  God presides in the divine assembly;He renders judgment among the gods: A Psalm of Asaph.
2.  “How long will you judge unjustlyand show partiality to the wicked?SelahA Psalm of Asaph.
3.  Defend the cause of the weak and fatherless;uphold the rights of the afflicted and oppressed.A Psalm of Asaph.
4.  Rescue the weak and needy;save them from the hand of the wicked.A Psalm of Asaph.
5.  They do not know or understand;they wander in the darkness;all the foundations of the earth are shaken.A Psalm of Asaph.
6.  I have said, ‘You are gods;you are all sons of the Most High.’A Psalm of Asaph.
7.  But like mortals you will die,and like rulers you will fall.” A Psalm of Asaph.
8.  Arise, O God, judge the earth,for all the nations are Your inheritance.
