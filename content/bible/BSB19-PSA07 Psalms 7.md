2.  or they will shred my soul like a lionand tear me to pieces with no one to rescue me. A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
3.  O LORD my God, if I have done this,if injustice is on my hands,A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
4.  if I have rewarded my ally with evil,if I have plundered my foe without cause,A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
5.  then may my enemy pursue me and overtake me;may he trample me to the groundand leave my honor in the dust.SelahA Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
6.  Arise, O LORD, in Your anger;rise up against the fury of my enemies.Awake, my God, and ordain judgment.A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
7.  Let the assembled peoples gather around You;take Your seat over them on high.A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
8.  The LORD judges the peoples;vindicate me, O LORD,according to my righteousness and integrity.A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
9.  Put an end to the evil of the wicked,but establish the righteous,O righteous God who searches hearts and minds. A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
10. My shield is with God,who saves the upright in heart.A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
11. God is a righteous judgeand a God who feels indignation each day.A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
12. If one does not repent,God will sharpen His sword;He has bent and strung His bow.A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
13. He has prepared His deadly weapons;He ordains His arrows with fire. A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
14. Behold, the wicked man travails with evil;he conceives trouble and births falsehood.A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
15. He has dug a hole and hollowed it out;he has fallen into a pit of his own making.A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
16. His trouble recoils on himself,and his violence falls on his own head. A Shiggaion of David, which he sang to the LORD concerning the words of Cush, a Benjamite.
17. I will thank the LORD for His righteousnessand sing praise to the name of the LORD Most High.
