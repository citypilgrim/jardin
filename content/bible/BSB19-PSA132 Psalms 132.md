1.  O LORD, remember on behalf of Davidall the hardships he endured,A song of ascents.
2.  how he swore an oath to the LORD,and vowed to the Mighty One of Jacob:A song of ascents.
3.  “I will not enter my houseor get into my bed,A song of ascents.
4.  I will not give sleep to my eyesor slumber to my eyelids,A song of ascents.
5.  until I find a place for the LORD,a dwelling for the Mighty One of Jacob.” A song of ascents.
6.  We heard that the ark was in Ephrathah;we found it in the fields of Jaar.A song of ascents.
7.  Let us go to His dwelling place;let us worship at His footstool.A song of ascents.
8.  Arise, O LORD, to Your resting place,You and the ark of Your strength.A song of ascents.
9.  May Your priests be clothed with righteousness,and Your saints shout for joy.A song of ascents.
10. For the sake of Your servant David,do not reject Your anointed one. A song of ascents.
11. The LORD swore an oath to David,a promise He will not revoke:“One of your descendantsI will place on your throne.A song of ascents.
12. If your sons keep My covenantand the testimony I will teach them,then their sons will also sit on your throneforever and ever.” A song of ascents.
13. For the LORD has chosen Zion;He has desired it for His home:A song of ascents.
14. “This is My resting place forever and ever;here I will dwell, for I have desired this home.A song of ascents.
15. I will bless her with abundant provisions;I will satisfy her poor with bread.A song of ascents.
16. I will clothe her priests with salvation,and her saints will sing out in joy.A song of ascents.
17. There I will make a horn grow for David;I have prepared a lamp for My anointed one.A song of ascents.
18. I will clothe his enemies with shame,but the crown upon him will gleam.”
