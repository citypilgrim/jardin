1.  O God, be not silent; be not speechless;be not still, O God.A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
2.  See how Your enemies rage,how Your foes have reared their heads.A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
3.  With cunning they scheme against Your peopleand conspire against those You cherish,A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
4.  saying, “Come, let us erase them as a nation;may the name of Israel be remembered no more.” A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
5.  For with one mind they plot together,they form an alliance against You—A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
6.  the tents of Edom and the Ishmaelites,of Moab and the Hagrites,A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
7.  of Gebal, Ammon, and Amalek,of Philistia with the people of Tyre.A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
8.  Even Assyria has joined them,lending strength to the sons of Lot.SelahA song. A Psalm of Asaph.
9.  Do to them as You did to Midian,as to Sisera and Jabin at the River Kishon,A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
10. who perished at Endorand became like dung on the ground.A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
11. Make their nobles like Oreb and Zeeb,and all their princes like Zebah and Zalmunna,A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
12. who said, “Let us possess for ourselvesthe pastures of God.” A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
13. Make them like tumbleweed, O my God,like chaff before the wind.A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
14. As fire consumes a forest,as a flame sets the mountains ablaze,A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
15. so pursue them with Your tempest,and terrify them with Your storm.A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
16. Cover their faces with shame,that they may seek Your name, O LORD. A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
17. May they be ever ashamed and terrified;may they perish in disgrace.A song. A Psalm of Asaph.
18. May they know that You alone,whose name is the LORD,are Most High over all the earth.
