1.  The earth is the LORD’s, and the fullness thereof,the world and all who dwell therein.A Psalm of David.
2.  For He has founded it upon the seasand established it upon the waters. A Psalm of David.
3.  Who may ascend the hill of the LORD?Who may stand in His holy place?A Psalm of David.
4.  He who has clean hands and a pure heart,who does not lift up his soul to an idolor swear deceitfully.A Psalm of David.
5.  He will receive blessing from the LORDand vindication from the God of his salvation.A Psalm of David.
6.  Such is the generation of those who seek Him,who seek Your face, O God of Jacob.SelahA Psalm of David.
7.  Lift up your heads, O gates!Be lifted up, O ancient doors,that the King of Glory may enter!A Psalm of David.
8.  Who is this King of Glory?The LORD strong and mighty,the LORD mighty in battle.A Psalm of David.
9.  Lift up your heads, O gates!Be lifted up, O ancient doors,that the King of Glory may enter!A Psalm of David.
10. Who is He, this King of Glory?The LORD of Hosts—He is the King of Glory.Selah
