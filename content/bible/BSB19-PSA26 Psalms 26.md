1.  Vindicate me, O LORD! For I have walked with integrity;I have trusted in the LORD without wavering.Of David.
2.  Test me, O LORD, and try me;examine my heart and mind.Of David.
3.  For Your loving devotion is before my eyes,and I have walked in Your truth. Of David.
4.  I do not sit with deceitful men,nor keep company with hypocrites.Of David.
5.  I hate the mob of evildoers,and refuse to sit with the wicked.Of David.
6.  I wash my hands in innocencethat I may go about Your altar, O LORD,Of David.
7.  to raise my voice in thanksgivingand declare all Your wonderful works. Of David.
8.  O LORD, I love the house where You dwell,the place where Your glory resides.Of David.
9.  Do not take my soul away with sinners,or my life with men of bloodshed,Of David.
10. in whose hands are wicked schemes,whose right hands are full of bribes. Of David.
11. But I will walk with integrity;redeem me and be merciful to me.Of David.
12. My feet stand on level ground;in the congregations I will bless the LORD.
