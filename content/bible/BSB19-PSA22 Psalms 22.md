1.  My God, my God,why have You forsaken me?Why are You so far from saving me,so far from my words of groaning? For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
2.  I cry out by day, O my God,but You do not answer,and by night,but I have no rest.  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
3.  Yet You are holy,enthroned on the praises of Israel. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
4.  In You our fathers trusted;they trusted and You delivered them. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
5.  They cried out to You and were set free;they trusted in You and were not disappointed.  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
6.  But I am a worm and not a man,scorned by men and despised by the people. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
7.  All who see me mock me;they sneer and shake their heads: For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
8.  “He trusts in the LORD,let the LORD deliver him;let the LORD rescue him,since He delights in him.”  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
9.  Yet You brought me forth from the womb;You made me secure at my mother’s breast. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
10. From birth I was cast upon You;from my mother’s womb You have been my God. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
11. Be not far from me,for trouble is nearand there is no one to help.  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
12. Many bulls surround me;strong bulls of Bashan encircle me. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
13. They open their jaws against melike lions that roar and maul. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
14. I am poured out like water,and all my bones are disjointed.My heart is like wax;it melts away within me. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
15. My strength is dried up like a potsherd,and my tongue sticks to the roof of my mouth.You lay me in the dust of death.  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
16. For dogs surround me;a band of evil men encircles me;they have pierced my hands and feet. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
17. I can count all my bones;they stare and gloat over me. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
18. They divide my garments among themand cast lots for my clothing.  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
19. But You, O LORD, be not far off;O my Strength, come quickly to help me. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
20. Deliver my soul from the sword,my precious life from the power of wild dogs. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
21. Save me from the mouth of the lion;at the horns of the wild oxen You have answered me!  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
22. I will proclaim Your name to my brothers;I will praise You in the assembly. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
23. You who fear the LORD, praise Him!All descendants of Jacob, honor Him!All offspring of Israel, revere Him! For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
24. For He has not despised or detestedthe torment of the afflicted.He has not hidden His face from him,but has attended to his cry for help.  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
25. My praise for You resounds in the great assembly;I will fulfill my vows before those who fear You. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
26. The poor will eat and be satisfied;those who seek the LORD will praise Him.May your hearts live forever!  For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
27. All the ends of the earthwill remember and turn to the LORD.All the families of the nationswill bow down before Him. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
28. For dominion belongs to the LORDand He rules over the nations. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
29. All the rich of the earth will feast and worship;all who go down to the dust will kneel before Him—even those unable to preserve their lives. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
30. Posterity will serve Him;they will declare the Lord to a new generation. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawn.” A Psalm of David.
31. They will come and proclaim His righteousnessto a people yet unborn—all that He has done.
