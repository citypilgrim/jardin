1.  Hear my cry, O God;attend to my prayer.For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. Of David.
2.  From the ends of the earth I call out to Youwhenever my heart is faint.Lead me to the rockthat is higher than I.For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. Of David.
3.  For You have been my refuge,a tower of strength against the enemy.For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. Of David.
4.  Let me dwell in Your tent foreverand take refuge in the shelter of Your wings.SelahFor the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. Of David.
5.  For You have heard my vows, O God;You have given me the inheritancereserved for those who fear Your name. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. Of David.
6.  Increase the days of the king’s life;may his years span many generations.For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. Of David.
7.  May he sit enthroned in God’s presence forever;appoint Your loving devotionand Your faithfulness to guard him.For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. Of David.
8.  Then I will ever sing praise to Your nameand fulfill my vows day by day.
