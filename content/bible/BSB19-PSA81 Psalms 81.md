1.  Sing for joy to God our strength;make a joyful noise to the God of Jacob.For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
2.  Lift up a song, strike the tambourine,play the sweet-sounding harp and lyre.For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
3.  Sound the ram’s horn at the New Moon,and at the full moon on the day of our Feast.For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
4.  For this is a statute for Israel,an ordinance of the God of Jacob.For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
5.  He ordained it as a testimony for Josephwhen he went out over the land of Egypt,where I heard an unfamiliar language: For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
6.  “I relieved his shoulder of the burden;his hands were freed from the basket.For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
7.  You called out in distress, and I rescued you;I answered you from the cloud of thunder;I tested you at the waters of Meribah.SelahFor the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
8.  Hear, O My people, and I will warn you:O Israel, if only you would listen to Me!For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
9.  There must be no strange god among you,nor shall you bow to a foreign god.For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
10. I am the LORD your God,who brought you up out of Egypt.Open wide your mouth,and I will fill it. For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
11. But My people would not listen to Me,and Israel would not obey Me.For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
12. So I gave them up to their stubborn heartsto follow their own devices.For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
13. If only My people would listen to Me,if Israel would follow My ways,For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
14. how soon I would subdue their enemiesand turn My hand against their foes!For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
15. Those who hate the LORD would feign obedience,and their doom would last forever.For the choirmaster. According to Gittith. Of Asaph.
16. But I would feed you the finest wheat;with honey from the rock I would satisfy you.”
