1.  O LORD, the God of my salvation,day and night I cry out before You.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
2.  May my prayer come before You;incline Your ear to my cry.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
3.  For my soul is full of troubles,and my life draws near to Sheol.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
4.  I am counted among those descending to the Pit.I am like a man without strength. A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
5.  I am forsaken among the dead,like the slain who lie in the grave,whom You remember no more,who are cut off from Your care. A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
6.  You have laid me in the lowest Pit,in the darkest of the depths.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
7.  Your wrath weighs heavily upon me;all Your waves have submerged me.SelahA song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
8.  You have removed my friends from me;You have made me repulsive to them;I am confined and cannot escape.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
9.  My eyes grow dim with grief.I call to You daily, O LORD;I spread out my hands to You.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
10. Do You work wonders for the dead?Do departed spirits rise up to praise You?SelahA song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
11. Can Your loving devotion be proclaimed in the grave,Your faithfulness in Abaddon?A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
12. Will Your wonders be known in the darkness,or Your righteousness in the land of oblivion? A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
13. But to You, O LORD, I cry for help;in the morning my prayer comes before You.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
14. Why, O LORD, do You reject me?Why do You hide Your face from me? A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
15. From my youth I was afflicted and near death.I have borne Your terrors; I am in despair.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
16. Your wrath has swept over me;Your terrors have destroyed me.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
17. All day long they engulf me like water;they enclose me on every side.A song. A Psalm of the sons of Korah. For the choirmaster. According to Mahalath Leannoth. A Maskil of Heman the Ezrahite.
18. You have removed my beloved and my friend;darkness is my closest companion.
