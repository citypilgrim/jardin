1.  O LORD, do not rebuke me in Your angeror discipline me in Your wrath. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
2.  For Your arrows have pierced me deeply,and Your hand has pressed down on me.  A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
3.  There is no soundness in my bodybecause of Your anger;there is no rest in my bonesbecause of my sin. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
4.  For my iniquities have overwhelmed me;they are a burden too heavy to bear. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
5.  My wounds are foul and festeringbecause of my sinful folly. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
6.  I am bent and brought low;all day long I go about mourning. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
7.  For my loins are full of burning pain,and no soundness remains in my body. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
8.  I am numb and badly crushed;I groan in anguish of heart.  A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
9.  O Lord, my every desire is before You;my groaning is not hidden from You. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
10. My heart pounds, my strength fails,and even the light of my eyes has faded. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
11. My beloved and friends shun my disease,and my kinsmen stand at a distance. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
12. Those who seek my life lay snares;those who wish me harm speak destruction,plotting deceit all day long.  A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
13. But like a deaf man, I do not hear;and like a mute man, I do not open my mouth. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
14. I am like a man who cannot hear,whose mouth offers no reply. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
15. I wait for You, O LORD;You will answer, O Lord my God. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
16. For I said, “Let them not gloat over me—those who taunt me when my foot slips.”  A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
17. For I am ready to fall,and my pain is ever with me. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
18. Yes, I confess my iniquity;I am troubled by my sin. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
19. Many are my enemies without cause,and many hate me without reason. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
20. Those who repay my good with evilattack me for pursuing the good.  A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
21. Do not forsake me, O LORD;be not far from me, O my God. A Psalm of David, for remembrance.
22. Come quickly to help me,O Lord my Savior.
