1.  I will sing of the loving devotion of the LORD forever;with my mouth I will proclaim Your faithfulness to all generations.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
2.  For I have said, “Loving devotion is built up forever;in the heavens You establish Your faithfulness.” A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
3.  You said, “I have made a covenant with My chosen one,I have sworn to David My servant:A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
4.  ‘I will establish your offspring foreverand build up your throne for all generations.’”SelahA Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
5.  The heavens praise Your wonders, O LORD—Your faithfulness as well—in the assembly of the holy ones.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
6.  For who in the skies can compare with the LORD?Who among the heavenly beings is like the LORD?A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
7.  In the council of the holy ones, God is greatly feared,and awesome above all who surround Him. A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
8.  O LORD God of Hosts, who is like You?O mighty LORD, Your faithfulness surrounds You.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
9.  You rule the raging sea;when its waves mount up, You still them.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
10. You crushed Rahab like a carcass;You scattered Your enemies with Your mighty arm.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
11. The heavens are Yours, and also the earth.The earth and its fullness You founded.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
12. North and south You created;Tabor and Hermon shout for joy at Your name. A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
13. Mighty is Your arm; strong is Your hand.Your right hand is exalted.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
14. Righteousness and justice are the foundation of Your throne;loving devotion and faithfulness go before You. A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
15. Blessed are those who know the joyful sound,who walk, O LORD, in the light of Your presence.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
16. They rejoice in Your name all day long,and in Your righteousness they exult.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
17. For You are the glory of their strength,and by Your favor our horn is exalted.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
18. Surely our shield belongs to the LORD,and our king to the Holy One of Israel. A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
19. You once spoke in a vision;to Your godly ones You said,“I have bestowed help on a warrior;I have exalted one chosen from the people.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
20. I have found My servant David;with My sacred oil I have anointed him.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
21. My hand will sustain him;surely My arm will strengthen him. A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
22. No enemy will exact tribute;no wicked man will oppress him.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
23. I will crush his foes before himand strike down those who hate him.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
24. My faithfulness and loving devotion will be with him,and through My name his horn will be exalted.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
25. I will set his hand over the sea,and his right hand upon the rivers.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
26. He will call to Me, ‘You are my Father,my God, the Rock of my salvation.’ A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
27. I will indeed appoint him as My firstborn,the highest of the kings of the earth.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
28. I will forever preserve My loving devotion for him,and My covenant with him will stand fast.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
29. I will establish his line forever,his throne as long as the heavens endure. A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
30. If his sons forsake My lawand do not walk in My judgments,A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
31. if they violate My statutesand fail to keep My commandments,A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
32. I will attend to their transgression with the rod,and to their iniquity with stripes. A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
33. But I will not withdraw My loving devotion from him,nor ever betray My faithfulness.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
34. I will not violate My covenantor alter the utterance of My lips.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
35. Once and for all I have sworn by My holiness—I will not lie to David—A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
36. his offspring shall endure forever,and his throne before Me like the sun,A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
37. like the moon, established forever,a faithful witness in the sky.”SelahA Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
38. Now, however, You have spurned and rejected him;You are enraged by Your anointed one.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
39. You have renounced the covenant with Your servantand sullied his crown in the dust.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
40. You have broken down all his walls;You have reduced his strongholds to rubble.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
41. All who pass by plunder him;he has become a reproach to his neighbors. A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
42. You have exalted the right hand of his foes;You have made all his enemies rejoice.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
43. You have bent the edge of his swordand have not sustained him in battle.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
44. You have ended his splendorand cast his throne to the ground.A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
45. You have cut short the days of his youth;You have covered him with shame.SelahA Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
46. How long, O LORD?Will You hide Yourself forever?Will Your wrath keep burning like fire?A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
47. Remember the briefness of my lifespan!For what futility You have created all men!A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
48. What man can live and never see death?Can he deliver his soul from the power of Sheol?SelahA Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
49. Where, O Lord, is Your loving devotion of old,which You faithfully swore to David?A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
50. Remember, O Lord, the reproach of Your servants,which I bear in my heart from so many people—A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
51. how Your enemies have taunted, O LORD,and have mocked every step of Your anointed one! A Maskil of Ethan the Ezrahite.
52. Blessed be the LORD forever! Amen and amen.
