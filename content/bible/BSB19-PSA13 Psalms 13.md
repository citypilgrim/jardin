1.  How long, O LORD?Will You forget me forever?How long will You hide Your face from me?For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
2.  How long must I wrestle in my soul,with sorrow in my heart each day?How long will my enemy dominate me? For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
3.  Consider me and respond, O LORD my God.Give light to my eyes, lest I sleep in death,For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
4.  lest my enemy say, “I have overcome him,”and my foes rejoice when I fall. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
5.  But I have trusted in Your loving devotion;my heart will rejoice in Your salvation.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
6.  I will sing to the LORD,for He has been good to me.
