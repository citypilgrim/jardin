1.  The LORD said to my Lord:“Sit at My right handuntil I make Your enemiesa footstool for Your feet.”  A Psalm of David.
2.  The LORD extends Your mighty scepter from Zion:“Rule in the midst of Your enemies.” A Psalm of David.
3.  Your people shall be willingon Your day of battle.Arrayed in holy splendor, from the womb of the dawn,to You belongs the dew of Your youth.  A Psalm of David.
4.  The LORD has swornand will not change His mind:“You are a priest foreverin the order of Melchizedek.”  A Psalm of David.
5.  The Lord is at Your right hand;He will crush kings in the day of His wrath. A Psalm of David.
6.  He will judge the nations, heaping up the dead;He will crush the leaders far and wide. A Psalm of David.
7.  He will drink from the brook by the road;therefore He will lift up His head.
