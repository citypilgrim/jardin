1.  Preserve me, O God,for in You I take refuge.  A Miktam of David.
2.  I said to the LORD, “You are my Lord;apart from You I have no good thing.” A Miktam of David.
3.  As for the saints in the land,they are the excellencein whom all my delight resides. A Miktam of David.
4.  Sorrows will multiplyto those who chase other gods.I will not pour out their libations of blood,or speak their names with my lips.  A Miktam of David.
5.  The LORD is my chosen portion and my cup;You have made my lot secure. A Miktam of David.
6.  The lines of my boundary have fallen in pleasant places;surely my inheritance is delightful. A Miktam of David.
7.  I will bless the LORD who counsels me;even at night my conscience instructs me.  A Miktam of David.
8.  I have set the LORD always before me.Because He is at my right hand, I will not be shaken. A Miktam of David.
9.  Therefore my heart is glad and my tongue rejoices;my body also will dwell securely. A Miktam of David.
10. For You will not abandon my soul to Sheol,nor will You let Your Holy One see decay. A Miktam of David.
11. You have made known to me the path of life;You will fill me with joy in Your presence,with eternal pleasures at Your right hand.
