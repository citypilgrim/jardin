1.  Listen to my prayer, O God,and do not ignore my plea. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
2.  Attend to me and answer me.I am restless in my complaint,and distraught For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
3.  at the voice of the enemy,at the pressure of the wicked.For they release disaster upon meand revile me in their anger.  For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
4.  My heart murmurs within me,and the terrors of death assail me. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
5.  Fear and trembling grip me,and horror has overwhelmed me. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
6.  I said, “Oh, that I had wings like a dove!I would fly away and find rest. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
7.  How far away I would flee!In the wilderness I would remain.Selah For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
8.  I would hurry to my shelter,far from this raging tempest.”  For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
9.  O Lord, confuse and confound their speech,for I see violence and strife in the city. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
10. Day and night they encircle the walls,while malice and trouble lie within. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
11. Destruction is within;oppression and deceit never leave the streets.  For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
12. For it is not an enemy who insults me;that I could endure.It is not a foe who rises against me;from him I could hide. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
13. But it is you, a man like myself,my companion and close friend. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
14. We shared sweet fellowship together;we walked with the crowd into the house of God.  For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
15. Let death seize them by surprise;let them go down to Sheol alive,for evil is with them in their homes. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
16. But I call to God,and the LORD saves me. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
17. Morning, noon, and night, I cry out in distress,and He hears my voice. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
18. He redeems my soul in peacefrom the battle waged against me,even though many oppose me. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
19. God will hear and humiliate them—the One enthroned for the ages—Selahbecause they do not changeand they have no fear of God.  For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
20. My companion attacks his friends;he violates his covenant. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
21. His speech is smooth as butter,but war is in his heart.His words are softer than oil,yet they are swords unsheathed.  For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
22. Cast your burden upon the LORDand He will sustain you;He will never let the righteous be shaken. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David.
23. But You, O God, will bring them downto the Pit of destruction;men of bloodshed and deceitwill not live out half their days. But I will trust in You.
