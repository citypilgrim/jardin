1.  We give thanks to You, O God;we give thanks, for Your Name is near.The people declare Your wondrous works.  For the choirmaster: To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
2.  “When I choose a time,I will judge fairly. For the choirmaster: To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
3.  When the earth and all its dwellers quake,it is I who bear up its pillars.Selah For the choirmaster: To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
4.  I say to the proud, ‘Do not boast,’and to the wicked, ‘Do not lift up your horn. For the choirmaster: To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
5.  Do not lift up your horn against heavenor speak with an outstretched neck.’”  For the choirmaster: To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
6.  For exaltation comes neither from east nor west,nor out of the desert, For the choirmaster: To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
7.  but it is God who judges;He brings down one and exalts another. For the choirmaster: To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
8.  For a cup is in the hand of the LORD,full of foaming wine mixed with spices.He pours from His cup,and all the wicked of the earthdrink it down to the dregs.  For the choirmaster: To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
9.  But I will proclaim Him forever;I will sing praise to the God of Jacob.  For the choirmaster: To the tune of “Do Not Destroy.” A Psalm of Asaph. A song.
10. “All the horns of the wicked I will cut off,but the horns of the righteous will be exalted.”
