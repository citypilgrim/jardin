1.  The Mighty One, God the LORD,speaks and summons the earthfrom where the sun rises to where it sets.A Psalm of Asaph.
2.  From Zion, perfect in beauty,God shines forth.A Psalm of Asaph.
3.  Our God approaches and will not be silent!Consuming fire precedes Him,and a tempest rages around Him.A Psalm of Asaph.
4.  He summons the heavens above,and the earth, that He may judge His people:A Psalm of Asaph.
5.  “Gather to Me My saints,who made a covenant with Me by sacrifice.”A Psalm of Asaph.
6.  And the heavens proclaim His righteousness,for God Himself is Judge.SelahA Psalm of Asaph.
7.  “Hear, O My people, and I will speak,O Israel, and I will testify against you:I am God, your God.A Psalm of Asaph.
8.  I do not rebuke you for your sacrifices,and your burnt offerings are ever before Me.A Psalm of Asaph.
9.  I have no need for a bull from your stallor goats from your pens,A Psalm of Asaph.
10. for every beast of the forest is Mine—the cattle on a thousand hills.A Psalm of Asaph.
11. I know every bird in the mountains,and the creatures of the field are Mine.A Psalm of Asaph.
12. If I were hungry, I would not tell you,for the world is Mine, and the fullness thereof.A Psalm of Asaph.
13. Do I eat the flesh of bulls,or drink the blood of goats?A Psalm of Asaph.
14. Sacrifice a thank offering to God,and fulfill your vows to the Most High.A Psalm of Asaph.
15. Call upon Me in the day of trouble;I will deliver you, and you will honor Me.” A Psalm of Asaph.
16. To the wicked, however, God says,“What right have you to recite My statutesand to bear My covenant on your lips?A Psalm of Asaph.
17. For you hate My instructionand cast My words behind you.A Psalm of Asaph.
18. When you see a thief, you befriend him,and throw in your lot with adulterers.A Psalm of Asaph.
19. You unleash your mouth for eviland unharness your tongue for deceit.A Psalm of Asaph.
20. You sit and malign your brother;you slander your own mother’s son.A Psalm of Asaph.
21. You have done these things, and I kept silent;you thought I was just like you.But now I rebuke youand accuse you to your face. A Psalm of Asaph.
22. Now consider this, you who forget God,lest I tear you to pieces,with no one to rescue you:A Psalm of Asaph.
23. He who sacrifices a thank offering honors Me,and to him who rights his way,I will show the salvation of God.”
