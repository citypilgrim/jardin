1.  Save me, O God,for the waters are up to my neck.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
2.  I have sunk into the miry depths,where there is no footing;I have drifted into deep waters,where the flood engulfs me.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
3.  I am weary from my crying;my throat is parched.My eyes fail,looking for my God.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
4.  Those who hate me without causeoutnumber the hairs of my head;many are those who would destroy me—my enemies for no reason.Though I did not steal,I must repay. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
5.  You know my folly, O God,and my guilt is not hidden from You.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
6.  May those who hope in You not be ashamed through me,O Lord GOD of Hosts;may those who seek You not be dishonored through me,O God of Israel.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
7.  For I have endured scorn for Your sake,and shame has covered my face.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
8.  I have become a stranger to my brothersand a foreigner to my mother’s sons,For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
9.  because zeal for Your house has consumed me,and the insults of those who insult You have fallen on me.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
10. I wept and fasted,but it brought me reproach.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
11. I made sackcloth my clothing,and I was sport to them.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
12. Those who sit at the gate mock me,and I am the song of drunkards. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
13. But my prayer to You, O LORD,is for a time of favor.In Your abundant loving devotion, O God,answer me with Your sure salvation.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
14. Rescue me from the mireand do not let me sink;deliver me from my foesand out of the deep waters.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
15. Do not let the floods engulf meor the depths swallow me up;let not the Pit close its mouth over me.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
16. Answer me, O LORD,for Your loving devotion is good;turn to me in keeping with Your great compassion.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
17. Hide not Your face from Your servant,for I am in distress.Answer me quickly!For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
18. Draw near to my soul and redeem me;ransom me because of my foes. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
19. You know my reproach, my shame and disgrace.All my adversaries are before You.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
20. Insults have broken my heart,and I am in despair.I looked for sympathy, but there was none,for comforters, but I found no one.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
21. They poisoned my food with galland gave me vinegar to quench my thirst. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
22. May their table become a snare;may it be a retribution and a trap.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
23. May their eyes be darkened so they cannot see,and their backs be bent forever.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
24. Pour out Your wrath upon them,and let Your burning anger overtake them.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
25. May their place be deserted;let there be no one to dwell in their tents.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
26. For they persecute the one You struckand recount the pain of those You wounded.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
27. Add iniquity to their iniquity;let them not share in Your righteousness.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
28. May they be blotted out of the Book of Lifeand not listed with the righteous. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
29. But I am in pain and distress;let Your salvation protect me, O God.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
30. I will praise God’s name in songand exalt Him with thanksgiving.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
31. And this will please the LORD more than an ox,more than a bull with horns and hooves.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
32. The humble will see and rejoice.You who seek God, let your hearts be revived!For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
33. For the LORD listens to the needyand does not despise His captive people. For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
34. Let heaven and earth praise Him,the seas and everything that moves in them.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
35. For God will save Zionand rebuild the cities of Judah,that they may dwell there and possess it.For the choirmaster. To the tune of “Lilies.” Of David.
36. The descendants of His servants will inherit it,and those who love His name will settle in it.
