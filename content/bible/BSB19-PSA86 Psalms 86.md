1.  Incline Your ear, O LORD, and answer me,for I am poor and needy.A prayer of David.
2.  Preserve my soul, for I am godly.You are my God; save Your servant who trusts in You.A prayer of David.
3.  Be merciful to me, O Lord,for I call to You all day long.A prayer of David.
4.  Bring joy to Your servant,for to You, O Lord, I lift up my soul. A prayer of David.
5.  For You, O Lord, are kind and forgiving,rich in loving devotion to all who call on You.A prayer of David.
6.  Hear my prayer, O LORD,and attend to my plea for mercy.A prayer of David.
7.  In the day of my distress I call on You,because You answer me. A prayer of David.
8.  O Lord, there is none like You among the gods,nor any works like Yours.A prayer of David.
9.  All the nations You have madewill come and bow before You, O Lord,and they will glorify Your name.A prayer of David.
10. For You are great and perform wonders;You alone are God. A prayer of David.
11. Teach me Your way, O LORD,that I may walk in Your truth.Give me an undivided heart,that I may fear Your name.A prayer of David.
12. I will praise You, O Lord my God, with all my heart;I will glorify Your name forever.A prayer of David.
13. For great is Your loving devotion to me;You have delivered me from the depths of Sheol. A prayer of David.
14. The arrogant rise against me, O God;a band of ruthless men seeks my life,with no regard for You.A prayer of David.
15. But You, O Lord, are a compassionate and gracious God,slow to anger, abounding in loving devotion and faithfulness. A prayer of David.
16. Turn to me and have mercy;grant Your strength to Your servant;save the son of Your maidservant.A prayer of David.
17. Show me a sign of Your goodness,that my enemies may see and be ashamed;for You, O LORD, have helped me and comforted me.
