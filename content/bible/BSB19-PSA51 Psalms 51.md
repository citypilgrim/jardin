1.  Have mercy on me, O God,according to Your loving devotion;according to Your great compassion,blot out my transgressions. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
2.  Wash me clean of my iniquityand cleanse me from my sin. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
3.  For I know my transgressions,and my sin is always before me. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
4.  Against You, You only, have I sinnedand done what is evil in Your sight,so that You may be proved right when You speakand blameless when You judge. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
5.  Surely I was brought forth in iniquity;I was sinful when my mother conceived me.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
6.  Surely You desire truth in the inmost being;You teach me wisdom in the inmost place. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
7.  Purify me with hyssop, and I will be clean;wash me, and I will be whiter than snow. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
8.  Let me hear joy and gladness;let the bones You have crushed rejoice. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
9.  Hide Your face from my sinsand blot out all my iniquities.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
10. Create in me a clean heart, O God,and renew a right spirit within me. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
11. Cast me not away from Your presence;take not Your Holy Spirit from me. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
12. Restore to me the joy of Your salvation,and sustain me with a willing spirit. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
13. Then I will teach transgressors Your ways,and sinners will return to You.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
14. Deliver me from bloodguilt, O God,the God of my salvation,and my tongue will sing of Your righteousness. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
15. O Lord, open my lips,and my mouth will declare Your praise. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
16. For You do not delight in sacrifice, or I would bring it;You take no pleasure in burnt offerings. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
17. The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit;a broken and a contrite heart,O God, You will not despise.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
18. In Your good pleasure, cause Zion to prosper;build up the walls of Jerusalem. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. When Nathan the prophet came to him after his adultery with Bathsheba.
19. Then You will delight in righteous sacrifices,in whole burnt offerings;then bulls will be offered on Your altar.
