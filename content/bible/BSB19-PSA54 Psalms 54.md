1.  Save me, O God, by Your name,and vindicate me by Your might! For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David. When the Ziphites went to Saul and said, “Is David not hiding among us?”
2.  Hear my prayer, O God;listen to the words of my mouth. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David. When the Ziphites went to Saul and said, “Is David not hiding among us?”
3.  For strangers rise up against me,and ruthless men seek my life—men with no regard for God.Selah For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David. When the Ziphites went to Saul and said, “Is David not hiding among us?”
4.  Surely God is my helper;the Lord is the sustainer of my soul. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David. When the Ziphites went to Saul and said, “Is David not hiding among us?”
5.  He will reward my enemies with evil.In Your faithfulness, destroy them. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David. When the Ziphites went to Saul and said, “Is David not hiding among us?”
6.  Freely I will sacrifice to You;I will praise Your name, O LORD, for it is good. For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. A Maskil of David. When the Ziphites went to Saul and said, “Is David not hiding among us?”
7.  For He has delivered me from every trouble,and my eyes have stared down my foes.
