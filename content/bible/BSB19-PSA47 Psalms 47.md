1.  Clap your hands, all you peoples;shout unto God with a voice of triumph. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
2.  How awesome is the LORD Most High,the great King over all the earth!For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
3.  He subdues nations beneath us,and peoples under our feet.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
4.  He chooses our inheritance for us,the pride of Jacob, whom He loves.SelahFor the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
5.  God has ascended amid shouts of joy,the LORD with the sound of the horn.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
6.  Sing praises to God, sing praises;sing praises to our King, sing praises!For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
7.  For God is King of all the earth;sing profound praises to Him. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
8.  God reigns over the nations;God is seated on His holy throne.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.
9.  The nobles of the nations have assembledas the people of the God of Abraham;for the shields of the earth belong to God;He is highly exalted.
