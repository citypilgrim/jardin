1.  In God alone my soul finds rest;my salvation comes from Him.For the choirmaster. According to Jeduthun. A Psalm of David.
2.  He alone is my rock and my salvation.He is my fortress;I will never be shaken. For the choirmaster. According to Jeduthun. A Psalm of David.
3.  How long will you threaten a man?Will all of you throw him downlike a leaning wallor a tottering fence?For the choirmaster. According to Jeduthun. A Psalm of David.
4.  They fully intend to cast him down from his lofty perch;they delight in lies;with their mouths they bless,but inwardly they curse.SelahFor the choirmaster. According to Jeduthun. A Psalm of David.
5.  Rest in God alone, O my soul,for my hope comes from Him.For the choirmaster. According to Jeduthun. A Psalm of David.
6.  He alone is my rock and my salvation;He is my fortress; I will not be shaken.For the choirmaster. According to Jeduthun. A Psalm of David.
7.  My salvation and my honor rest on God, my strong rock;my refuge is in God.For the choirmaster. According to Jeduthun. A Psalm of David.
8.  Trust in Him at all times, O people;pour out your hearts before Him.God is our refuge.SelahFor the choirmaster. According to Jeduthun. A Psalm of David.
9.  Lowborn men are but a vapor,the exalted but a lie.Weighed on the scale, they go up;together they are but a vapor.For the choirmaster. According to Jeduthun. A Psalm of David.
10. Place no trust in extortion,or false hope in stolen goods.If your riches increase,do not set your heart upon them. For the choirmaster. According to Jeduthun. A Psalm of David.
11. God has spoken once;I have heard this twice:that power belongs to God,For the choirmaster. According to Jeduthun. A Psalm of David.
12. and loving devotion to You, O Lord.For You will repay each manaccording to his deeds.
