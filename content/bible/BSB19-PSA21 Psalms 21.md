1.  O LORD, the king rejoices in Your strength.How greatly he exults in Your salvation! For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
2.  You have granted his heart’s desireand have not withheld the request of his lips.Selah For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
3.  For You welcomed him with rich blessings;You placed on his head a crown of pure gold. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
4.  He asked You for life, and You granted it—length of days, forever and ever.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
5.  Great is his glory in Your salvation;You bestow on him splendor and majesty. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
6.  For You grant him blessings forever;You cheer him with joy in Your presence. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
7.  For the king trusts in the LORD;through the loving devotion of the Most High,he will not be shaken.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
8.  Your hand will apprehend all Your enemies;Your right hand will seize those who hate You. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
9.  You will place them in a fiery furnaceat the time of Your appearing. In His wrath the LORD will engulf them,and the fire will consume them. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
10. You will wipe their descendants from the earth,and their offspring from the sons of men.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
11. Though they intend You harm,the schemes they devise will not prevail. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
12. For You will put them to flightwhen Your bow is trained upon them.  For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.
13. Be exalted, O LORD, in Your strength;we will sing and praise Your power.
