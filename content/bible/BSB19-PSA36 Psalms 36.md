1.  An oracle is in my heartregarding the transgression of the wicked man:There is no fear of Godbefore his eyes.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David, the servant of the LORD.
2.  For his eyes are too full of conceitto detect or hate his own sin.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David, the servant of the LORD.
3.  The words of his mouth are wicked and deceitful;he has ceased to be wise and well-doing.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David, the servant of the LORD.
4.  Even on his bed he plots wickedness;he sets himself on a path that is not good;he fails to reject evil. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David, the servant of the LORD.
5.  Your loving devotion, O LORD, reaches to the heavens,Your faithfulness to the clouds.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David, the servant of the LORD.
6.  Your righteousness is like the highest mountains;Your judgments are like the deepest sea.O LORD, You preserve man and beast.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David, the servant of the LORD.
7.  How precious is Your loving devotion, O God,that the children of men take refugein the shadow of Your wings!For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David, the servant of the LORD.
8.  They feast on the abundance of Your house,and You give them drink from Your river of delights.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David, the servant of the LORD.
9.  For with You is the fountain of life;in Your light we see light. For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David, the servant of the LORD.
10. Extend Your loving devotion to those who know You,and Your righteousness to the upright in heart.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David, the servant of the LORD.
11. Let not the foot of the proud come against me,nor the hand of the wicked drive me away.For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David, the servant of the LORD.
12. There the evildoers lie fallen,thrown down and unable to rise.
