1.  Why have You rejected us forever, O God?Why does Your anger smolderagainst the sheep of Your pasture? A Maskil of Asaph.
2.  Remember Your congregation,which You purchased long agoand redeemed as the tribe of Your inheritance—Mount Zion where You dwell. A Maskil of Asaph.
3.  Turn Your steps to the everlasting ruins,to everything in the sanctuary the enemy has destroyed.  A Maskil of Asaph.
4.  Your foes have roared within Your meeting place;they have unfurled their banners as signs, A Maskil of Asaph.
5.  like men wielding axes in a thicket of trees A Maskil of Asaph.
6.  and smashing all the carvings with hatchets and picks. A Maskil of Asaph.
7.  They have burned Your sanctuary to the ground;they have defiled the dwelling place of Your Name.  A Maskil of Asaph.
8.  They said in their hearts,“We will crush them completely.”They burned down every placewhere God met us in the land. A Maskil of Asaph.
9.  There are no signs for us to see.There is no longer any prophet.And none of us knows how long this will last. A Maskil of Asaph.
10. How long, O God, will the enemy taunt You?Will the foe revile Your name forever? A Maskil of Asaph.
11. Why do You withdraw Your strong right hand?Stretch it out to destroy them!  A Maskil of Asaph.
12. Yet God is my King from ancient times,working salvation on the earth. A Maskil of Asaph.
13. You divided the sea by Your strength;You smashed the heads of the dragons of the sea; A Maskil of Asaph.
14. You crushed the heads of Leviathan;You fed him to the creatures of the desert. A Maskil of Asaph.
15. You broke open the fountain and the flood;You dried up the ever-flowing rivers. A Maskil of Asaph.
16. The day is Yours, and also the night;You established the moon and the sun. A Maskil of Asaph.
17. You set all the boundaries of the earth;You made the summer and winter.  A Maskil of Asaph.
18. Remember how the enemy has mocked You, O LORD,how a foolish people has spurned Your name. A Maskil of Asaph.
19. Do not deliver the soul of Your dove to beasts;do not forget the lives of Your afflicted forever. A Maskil of Asaph.
20. Consider Your covenant,for haunts of violence fill the dark places of the land. A Maskil of Asaph.
21. Do not let the oppressed retreat in shame;may the poor and needy praise Your name.  A Maskil of Asaph.
22. Rise up, O God; defend Your cause!Remember how the fool mocks You all day long. A Maskil of Asaph.
23. Do not disregard the clamor of Your adversaries,the uproar of Your enemies that ascends continually.
